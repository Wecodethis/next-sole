Refunds
If, for any reason you are unhappy with your purchase, we will happily provide you with a refund minus the shipping fee. Simply return your unwanted shoes to us unworn, in their original condition including original shoe box within 30 days from the date of dispatch together with a completed copy of our online returns form.

To return for a refund, simply:

  1. Pack up your unworn shoes into its original shoe box.
  2. Place the shoe box with the unworn shoes into another carton.
  3. Clearly label the carton with our return address.
  4. Clearly mark the external carton with your order number to ensure prompt processing of your return.
  5. Drop the carton off at your nearest post office for collection.

Returns may take up to 2-4 business days to reach our warehouse. Once we have received the return, your refund will be processed in 1-3 business days. Refunds are always processed and credited to the original method of payment at the time of purchase (Credit Card or PayPal) minus the shipping fee charged. Please also allow 3-5 business days for your financial institution to process the funds back into your account.
